About :)

Sidot is Tobi Awolaju, a graphic designer from Ileasa, Osun, Nigeria who does a variety of digital artwork including Illustrations, short films, Creative Commons VJ loops, everydays and VR / AR work. After he began releasing a set of widely used Creative Commons VJ loops he has worked on concert visuals for Davido, Wizkid, Burnerboy and many more. One of the originators of the current "365 auction" movement in Modern Art, he has been creating a picture everyday from start to finish and posting it online for over 2 years without missing a single day.
